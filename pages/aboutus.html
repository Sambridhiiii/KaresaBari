Our Story & Mission

KaresaBari is a smart vegetable gardening assistant designed for households and small-scale farmers in Nepal. Using AI, it can detect soil type from a simple photo and provide personalized guidance on which vegetables to grow based on soil, season, sunlight, and location. The platform also offers features like leaf disease detection, organic fertilizer recommendations, watering tips, and gardening tool suggestions.

Traditionally, kitchen gardening in Nepal relies on experience and trial-and-error, often resulting in poor crop selection and low productivity. KaresaBari makes gardening easy, reliable, and beginner-friendly, helping users grow healthy vegetables at home while saving time and maximizing yield.
