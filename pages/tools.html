🛠️ Tool Care Guide

  • Always clean your tools after use to prevent rust and disease.
  • Sharpen blades and edges regularly for efficient cutting.
  • Store in a dry place to extend tool lifespan.
  • Lubricate moving parts of pruning shears or sprayers monthly.
  • Inspect for damage before every use to ensure safety.
