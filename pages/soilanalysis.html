EXPERT RECOMMENDATIONS FOR YOUR SOIL TYPE

Improve Drainage

Mix in organic compost or sand to loosen the clay structure. This prevents waterlogging during monsoon and ensures roots can breathe.

grass

Mulching

Add a layer of dried leaves or straw to maintain a stable temperature and prevent the heavy soil from cracking during dry spells.

precision_manufacturing

Tilling Tip

Avoid tilling when the soil is very wet; it can lead to compaction that makes it even harder to work with in the future.
